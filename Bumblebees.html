Here in the UK we are blessed with 24 delightful species of bumblebee. These fluffy fliers belong to the order Hymenoptera, along with other bees, wasps and ants. Bumblebees are well known for their 'bumbling' flight and distinctive buzz - a treasured appearance of the British summer.
